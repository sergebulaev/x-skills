# The X Pillar Discipline

Pillars keep a week coherent without making it monotone. On X the default mix
leans harder into build-in-public and engagement than LinkedIn does, because the
audience skews toward builders and the platform rewards conversation.

## Default pillars

| Pillar | Share | What it is | Earns |
|---|---|---|---|
| Build-in-Public | 40% | real numbers, progress, mistakes from your own work | replies, bookmarks |
| Insight | 30% | contrarian takes, data points, teaching threads | reposts, bookmarks |
| Engagement | 20% | replies, quote tweets, relatable singles | replies, likes |
| Promotion | 10% | a launch, an offer, results that imply the product | varies |

Adjust the mix to the user's goal: a builder in launch mode might run 30/30/20/20.
Never let one pillar exceed 60% of the week.

## Why these pillars on X specifically

- **Build-in-Public** over-indexes on X. The founder/builder audience rewards
  raw transparency, and a real metric (good or ugly) is the easiest reply
  magnet you have.
- **Insight** is where bookmarks live. Lists, frameworks, data points, and "how
  I" threads get saved, and bookmarks are a heavy 2026 ranking signal.
- **Engagement** is a pillar, not a chore. Early growth on X comes more from
  replying well on other people's posts than from your own timeline. Budget
  daily reply time.
- **Promotion** is capped low. X punishes a timeline that reads like an ad.
  Prefer results that imply the product over direct pitches.

## Cadence

- **2-4 posts/day** is normal for an active X account and does not trigger the
  cannibalization penalty LinkedIn imposes on 2+/day.
- **Singles for presence, threads for depth.** A ~60/40 single-to-thread mix
  keeps you in the feed daily while still earning the bookmark-heavy threads.
- **Reply blocks daily.** 5-10 substantive replies a day on accounts in your
  niche compounds into profile visits and follows.

## Goal balance

Spread the week across replies, reposts, likes, and bookmarks. Bookmarks get a
weekly target of at least 2 because they are the most underrated lever: a
bookmark is a stronger quality signal than a like and rewards reference-worthy
content. See the root `references/hook-formulas.md` "Engagement-goal split" and
`references/algorithm-heuristics.md` "Bookmarks are the underrated lever".

## Anti-patterns

- All-bookmark-bait week (reads as a content farm).
- All-promotion week (kills trust fast on X).
- Zero reply budget (you post into the void).
- Repeating the same formula every day (the pattern becomes visible).
