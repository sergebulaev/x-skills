# Example: 7-Day X Plan

**Theme:** shipping an AI agent in public
**Audience:** indie builders, AI engineers, early founders
**Pillar mix:** 40% Build-in-Public / 30% Insight / 20% Engagement / 10% Promotion
**Cadence:** 2 posts/day weekdays, 1 on Saturday, reply block daily

## Daily reply targets

- **Who:** peer builders at 2-20k followers, AI founders shipping in public, the
  3-4 creators whose threads you keep bookmarking.
- **Templates:** R1 Answer-Their-Question, R2 Concede-Then-Sharpen, R3
  Extend-Their-Point, R5 Value-Add Quote.
- **Target:** 5-10 substantive replies/day. No "great thread!" filler.

## Balance check

- [x] Single-to-thread mix ~60/40 (7 singles, 4 threads + reply blocks)
- [x] At least 1 build-in-public post (Tue, Thu, Sat)
- [x] At least 1 bookmark-bait post (Tue x2, Wed, Thu)
- [x] At least 1 engagement/reply-heavy day (every day has a reply block)
- [x] No pillar over 60%
- [x] No formula repeated more than twice
- [x] Goal spread: replies (Mon PM, Thu AM, Fri PM), reposts (Mon AM, Wed PM),
      likes (Fri AM, Sat), bookmarks (Tue, Wed, Thu)

## Notes

- The Thu PM promotion post leans on X10's "results imply the product" shape so
  it does not read as an ad.
- Saturday's story thread (X8) is the one weekend slot, because story and
  relatable content travels on weekends when the build crowd is off the clock.
- Keep any product or launch link out of tweet 1. Drop it in a reply to your own
  thread so reach is not suppressed.
